Frequently Asked Questions

What is the return policy
Our return policy allows you to return items within 30 days of purchase for a full refund or exchange. Please refer to our Returns page for detailed instructions.
When will I recieve my order?
The delivery time depends on your location and the shipping method chosen. Typically, orders are processed within 1-2 business days, and standard shipping takes 3-5 business days.
Where are you located
Our headquarters is located in Stockholm, Sweden. However, we operate online, and our products are shipped globally from various distribution centers.
How can I track my order?
Once your order is shipped, you will receive a confirmation email with a tracking number. Use this number on our Track Order page to monitor the delivery status.
What payment methods do you accept?
We accept various payment methods, including credit/debit cards (Visa, MasterCard, American Express), PayPal, and other secure payment gateways such as Swish. Please visit our Payment Options page for details.
